## 👨‍💻 About Me

I am an AI researcher working at the intersection of **robot learning, reinforcement learning, computer vision, and embodied AI**.

- 🤖 Research Assistant at the **Cognitive Robotics Lab, University of Arkansas**
- 🧠 Developing hierarchical reinforcement learning and skill-blending methods for humanoid robots
- 🔬 Experienced in computer vision, vision-language models, dataset development, and model evaluation
- 👨‍🏫 Teaching Assistant for Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Computer Vision, NLP, and Reinforcement Learning
- 🌱 Currently exploring humanoid robotics, Deep RL, and intelligent embodied systems
